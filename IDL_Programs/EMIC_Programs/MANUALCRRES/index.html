My area of study is Space Physics. I am studying under scholarship as part of the Cooperative Research Centre for Satellite Systems (CRCSS).
The CRCSS will launch Australia's first satellite for thirty years in the year 2000, FEDSAT-1. The core partners in the CRCSS are the Commonwealth Scientific and Industrial Research Organisation Office of Space Science and Applications (CSIRO/COSSA), the University of South Australia, the Queensland University of Technology and The University of Newcastle Australia.
FEDSAT-1 will be a low Earth orbiting satellite, probably in a polar orbit and carrying space scientific package,communications systems and on-board computing systems. The University of Newcastle will contribute a magnetometer experiment to measure the Earth's main field and its variations.
